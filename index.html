FFA Community-Deathmatch Notes

  • When zoomedi n with awp your accuracy is good but unzooming and changing targets feels uncomfortable
  • Spraying at medium to long distances with rifles is very uncomfortable especially AK
  • Somtimes when you start shooting your crosshair is not on the enemies head which leads to a high amount of body shot kills
  • Sometimes you start shooting before your crosshair is on the enemy forcing you to correct the distance when spraying which is harder.
  • Your average crosshair placement when moving around is towards the chest/wasit instead of the head which requires you to flick upwards when going for headshots which increases your TTK
  • Sometimes your Counter-Strafing doesn't sync properly and you accidentally start shooting while you haven't stopped
